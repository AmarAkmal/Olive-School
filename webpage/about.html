THE OLIVE TREES SCHOOL About Our School

Dear Parents,

A very warm welcome to all parents, pupils, both old and new. we are happy that you have chosen to do your schooling here at tOts and we are confident as you read through this website you will get a glimpse of what it is like to be a learner at our highly vibrant, enriching and enjoyable Centre.

We hope to help prepare your children, to build not only to acquire knowledge academically but, we also offer your children the opportunity to develop useful skills to be independent and to nurture noble values that will make them a "valuable" independent person to your family, community and country.

We also place great importance in developing students' independence and communication skills through a wide range of internationally known learning technique together with tOts intervention module; with the aim to narrow the gap between your child and their age appropriate population.

We wish success and happiness for our children. Once again we are welcoming you and your children into our Centre. We hope a very rewarding partnership and closed family relationship is made over the time your child is with us.

Thank you for your trusts in The Olive Trees (tOts).
